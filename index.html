The Brand Kit pipeline

Five tools each make one piece of a brand — colors, icons, a card, a QR, packaged files. Every one exports to Brand Board, which composes them into a single shareable brand page you can hand to a client.

Brand Board Composes the whole kit Palette Studio Colors & fonts Icon Kit Favicons & social Digital Card Business card QR Creator Branded QR codes File Builder Packages the files

Each tool copies its work as a small file you paste into Brand Board — no accounts, nothing hosted. Save the finished board as one project file to reopen or revise later.

The networking pair

Two halves of one handshake. Hand out a polished card, capture everyone else's — they speak the same contact format, so a card made in one drops straight into the other.

Digital Card The card you hand out — branded, with a QR that saves your info to any phone. + Contact Capture Your rolodex — scan cards or QRs at an event and file people away with notes.

Scan a Digital Card QR in Contact Capture and the contact fills in for you. Contact Capture also syncs to Opsette, so captured people flow into your contacts, leads, or vendors.
